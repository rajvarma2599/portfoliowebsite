FAQ

As a UI/UX Designer & Developer, I offer comprehensive services including user research, wireframing, prototyping, UI design, UX testing, and front-end development to create intuitive and engaging digital experiences.

My design process follows a user-centered approach: research and discovery, ideation, prototyping, testing, and iteration. I conduct user interviews, create personas, build wireframes and high-fidelity prototypes in Figma, and validate designs through usability testing.

I primarily use Figma for design and prototyping. For development, I work with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and have experience in Python and Java. I also leverage tools like Google Analytics for user insights and various UX research methodologies.

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A simple landing page might take 2-4 weeks, while a full mobile app redesign could span 8-12 weeks, including research, design, prototyping, and testing phases.

To get started, reach out via the contact form or email. We'll discuss your project requirements, goals, and timeline. I prefer initial consultations to understand your vision and provide a tailored proposal.
