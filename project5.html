Social Media App Wireframe and Prototype

Overview: Developed wireframes and interactive prototypes for a new social networking platform targeting young professionals. Key Features

  • Wireframing and prototyping
  • Innovative UI patterns for connections
  • User testing and validation
  • Iterative design process
  • Intuitive user experience

Tools Used

Figma, Sketch, InVision, UserTesting.com

Results

Positive user feedback on connection features, high engagement in prototypes, and validated design decisions through testing.
