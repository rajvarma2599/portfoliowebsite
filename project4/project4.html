Landing Page for Tech Startup

A compelling landing page design that increased lead generation by 60% through trust-building elements and clear call-to-actions.

Project Overview

Crafted a compelling landing page for a fintech startup, emphasizing trust-building elements and clear call-to-actions. Utilized UX research methods to identify user pain points and designed a responsive layout that increased lead generation by 60% within the first month. The design focused on conversion optimization and user trust to drive business growth.

Key Features Implemented

Trust-Building Elements

Incorporated testimonials, certifications, and security badges.

Clear Call-to-Actions

Designed prominent CTAs to guide user actions.

UX Research & Pain Points

Identified and addressed key user challenges.

Responsive Design

Ensured optimal experience across all devices.

Lead Generation Optimization

Streamlined forms and processes for better conversions.

Tools & Technologies Used

Figma Adobe XD Hotjar Google Analytics

Results & Impact

Achieved a 60% increase in lead generation within the first month, improved user trust, and received positive feedback on design effectiveness. The landing page successfully converted visitors into leads and supported the startup's growth objectives.
